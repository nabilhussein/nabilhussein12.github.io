What to Do if You Know have Coivd-19?

Stay home except to get medical care.

  1. Most people with COVID-19 have mild illness and can recover at home without medical care
  2. Do not leave your home, except to get medical care
  3. Do not visit public areas
  4. Take care of yourself
  5. Get rest and stay hydrated
  6. Take over-the-counter medicines, such as acetaminophen, to help you feel better
  7. Stay in touch with your doctor
  8. Call before you get medical care.Be sure to get care if you have trouble breathing, or have any other emergency warning signs, or if you think it is an emergency
  9. Avoid public transportation, ride-sharing, or taxis
